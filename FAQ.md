## Learning Features

### How does spaced repetition work?

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that shows you words just before you're about to forget them. VocaVision uses the **SM-2 algorithm**:

1. **New word**: Review after 1 day
2. **Easy**: Review after 6 days
3. **Correct again**: Review after 15 days
4. **Continue**: Intervals increase exponentially

**Benefits**:
- Learn more efficiently
- Remember longer
- Study less, retain more
